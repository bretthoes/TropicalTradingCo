About us

Tropical Trading Co. is a small business dedicated to delivering Canada the best quality tropical foliage from Florida, year round.

We look for 3 attributes when choosing plants; premium quality, adaptability of the foliage, sustainability for the long distance travels.

​ We have something for both individual garden centres and large retailers.


What makes us unique




Tropicals are handpicked.


They are personally cared for in our greenhouse.


We have contacts both in Ontario and Florida.


Able to have a connection between both markets with constant availability updates.
